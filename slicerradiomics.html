slicer-Radiomics

Radiomics 3d Slicer extension

SlicerRadiomics is an extension for 3D Slicer that encapsulates pyradiomics library, which in turn implements calculation of a variety of radiomics features.

Open-source

Pending resolution of packaging issues, SlicerRadiomics is not currently distributed as an extension via the 3D Slicer ExtensionManager. You can however use this extension if you build SlicerRadiomics from source.
